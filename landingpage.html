ONCE IN A LIFETIME

This coming Summer, ORINEIN will be launching her latest single at the Eden Conservatory, to celebrate the blooming of the Queen Of The Night.

The ancient, sacred flower only blooms at the stroke of midnight, once every 50 years. This year, the blooming of the flower coincides with the upcoming super flower blood moon, making this an even rarer event of spiritual significance.

THE SET-UP

Housed in her own dedicated chapel, a whole observatory has been built around her so that the world can admire her rare beauty when she decides to unveil herself. This way, people have the opportunity to be graced by her presence whilst still keeping her safe from harm.

Orinein will guide you on a journey to another realm, as we break illusions of the past and welcome new, fruitful beginnings through the blessing from the Queen Of The Night, under the sacred glow of the lunar eclipse.
